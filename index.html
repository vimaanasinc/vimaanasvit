ACHIEVEMENTS

DEPARTMENTS

DESIGN
  • Analyzes requirements, constraints, and configures the aircraft.
  • Selects components, conducts stability analysis, and optimizes aerodynamics using software.
  • Balances trade-offs between performance, safety, and aesthetics.
  • Collaborates with other departments for integrated and efficient solutions.
    • XFLR Python 3.x
STRUCTURES
  • Focuses on 3D CAD modeling, structural integrity, and weight reduction.
  • Utilizes software like Solidworks and Fusion 360 for CAD modeling.
  • Employs ANSYS static structural analysis to ensure stability via size/material adjustments.
  • Implements Topology Optimization to identify stress-free areas, leading to aircraft weight reduction.
    • SOLIDWORKS ANSYS
CFD
  • Utilizes Computational Fluid Dynamics for fluid flow analysis.
  • Employs ANSYS Fluent to compute parameters like Lift, Drag, and Pressure.
  • Optimizes airfoil and fuselage design for reduced drag.
  • Enhances efficiency by ensuring optimal air flow.
  • Improves performance through simulation-based refinement.
    • ANSYS
AVIONICS
  • Manages electronic systems used on aircraft.
  • Selects motor, propeller, battery, ESC, and receiver based on requirements.
  • Conducts software analysis for optimal performance.
  • Performs experimental analysis for the best-suited drive train.
  • Collaborates with other departments for integrated solutions.
    • Microcontrollers
MANAGEMENT
  • Gets sponsors for the team and promotes the team actively.
  • Manages all documents and permissions needed for events and competitions.
  • Coordinates logistics for team participation in various events.
  • Plans and executes fundraising campaigns for project funding.
  • Fosters collaboration between different team departments.
    • CRM Tools
